OUR EXPERTISE

WHAT WE DO

At Giang Aluminum, we provide complete exterior solutions for residential and commercial properties across Ontario and the Greater Toronto Area (GTA). Our services include:

Siding Installation

Professional installation of Vinyl, Aluminum, Metal, and Fiber Cement (Hardie Board).

Cladding Solutions

Modern, energy-efficient exterior finishes for superior protection and beauty.

Soffit & Fascia

Roofline protection that ensures proper ventilation and prevents moisture damage.

Eavestrough & Leafguard

Custom water management systems to keep your foundation safe and debris-free.

Aluminum Capping

Maintenance-free aluminum capping for windows, doors, and trim protection.

Gutter Cleaning

Professional seasonal maintenance to remove debris and ensure your drainage system works perfectly.

Repairs & Maintenance

Fast and reliable exterior repairs for all siding and gutter issues.

At Giang Aluminum, we use high-quality materials and professional techniques to deliver long-lasting results with a clean, polished finish.
