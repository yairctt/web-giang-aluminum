OUR STORY

WHO WE ARE

At Giang Aluminum, we specialize in providing high-quality exterior siding solutions for residential and commercial projects across Ontario and the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

Our company was founded by Giovanni Morales and María Hernández, who bring over 7 years of experience in the industry. Their dedication, craftsmanship, and commitment to excellence have built a company focused on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

We offer professional installation services for vinyl siding, aluminum siding, cedar siding, fiber cement (Hardie), as well as soffit, fascia, and eavestrough systems. We use premium materials and modern techniques to ensure durable, visually appealing results that withstand Canada’s harsh weather conditions.

We are known for our punctuality, attention to detail, and strong work ethic. Every project is handled with care and precision, as if it were our own, ensuring results that exceed expectations.
